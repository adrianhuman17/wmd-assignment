About This Website

What Is Smoking Awareness BW?

An educational, awareness, and support platform focused on smoking prevention and health education in Botswana. We provide detailed information about smoking products, explain the health risks of active and passive smoking, and offer practical guidance for quitting. Accurate statistics are included throughout to reinforce key messages.
